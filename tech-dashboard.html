FY25/26 was the year CP+R’s technology stack came of age. David, operating as a one-person engineering team, delivered the payment portal, Hub CRM, and the full COO dashboard suite — three projects that would typically require a team. The roadmap for FY26/27 is ambitious but clear: Hub 2.0 takes the athlete experience digital, the food diary app adds a high-demand wellness feature, and security and compliance work ensures the infrastructure is enterprise-ready. The most operationally critical priority is RA compliance automation — the gap between 67.2% and the 90% target can only be closed with system-level prompts and enforcement, not manual process.
