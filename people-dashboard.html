Looking Ahead · FY26/27
The Commitment
Retention is the priority. Full stop.
Year one is the hardest. Every member of this team went through it — learning a new skill, in a new environment, to a new standard. That knowledge does not start over. It compounds. CP+R’s commitment going into FY26/27 is to invest in the people who built this foundation and give them the conditions to take it further.
The Opportunity
Year two is where the step change happens.
A sales team’s second year is consistently its breakout year. The systems are understood, the habits are forming, and the confidence is real. With James Porter leading the function and a team who have already proven they can build something from nothing, the platform for FY26/27 is stronger than it has ever been.
