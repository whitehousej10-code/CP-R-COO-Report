Annual Referral Volume Has Grown x7.7 Since FY18/19
Every financial year has delivered growth. Our last financial year was the strongest on record, with 967 referrals, 18.6 on average per week. Off target by 1.9 per week.
For most of the year we were fallng behind our FY 24/25 run rate. In the last 11 weeks I've prioritised partnerships and new relationships, helping us to fight back and exceed last year by +16 referrals.
Week 28, we set a CP+R record of 39 referrals. In May we did a record of 100 referrals.
When I look at the totals, I don't see a number, I think of the relationships, the phone calls, the last flexibility, their "constructive feedback", political management and energy applied to throughout the year. I also think of the increased competition we've felt this year and how we've continued to win.

Referral Seasonality
May, March and April are the three busiest months of the calander year consistently. 8 years of data show a clear spring surge and busy end to our financial year. This year, that was particularly challenging, simultanoeusly changing the infrastrucute of the company with you, developing a HR department and several coach exits testing our availibility.
June is statistically the quietest month of our year, a strategic month to reflect and deliver appraisals. Generally, referrals reflect school holidays and why we strategically booked Ellen's sabbatical for August which suited hers and the businesses needs.
