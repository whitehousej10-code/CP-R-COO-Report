CP+R generated £1,137,658 in new referral income this year — up 31.0% on FY24/25. Income per referral hit £1,178, a 25.3% improvement driven by stronger athlete quality and improved EM conversion. A pillar grew 53.9% to £800,555 — its strong final weeks (W50-52) adding £106k over the W49 position. C pillar more than doubled to £160,938. B pillar declined 27.5% to £176,166 — an honest reflection of insufficient consultant relationship investment, and a stated recovery priority for FY26/27.
