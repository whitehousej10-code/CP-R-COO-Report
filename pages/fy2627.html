A foundation year — in one paragraph

FY25/26 was the year the scaffolding went up across the whole organisation. Commercially, the portfolio grew 31% to £1.14M on a record 966 referrals, with the A pillar up 53.9% and the C self-referral pathway more than doubling. Clinically, we held NACR Green across all 7 KPIs and modernised the Assessment Centre. Operationally, we secured £600k+ in R&D relief, ran our first company-wide review, and moved from retrospective reporting to live, data-driven decisions. The team grew to 61, technology matured into six live systems and this very report became a working dashboard rather than a static PDF.

We were also honest about the gaps: the B pillar dipped 27.5%, EM conversion sat at 61.5% against a 64% target, RA compliance improved but incomplete, 317 Flown solos and huge change in infrastructure of the Cardiac Coaching Team and it's members. None of that is failure; it's the visible edge of a year-one team learning fast. The infrastructure is now in place. FY26/27 is about utilisation. We will remember this year as testing but hopefully for good reason. Digging a trench and laying the foundations of our hotel!
