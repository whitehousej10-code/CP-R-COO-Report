Key People Initiatives FY26/27
Retention: Five EMs go into FY26/27 with a full season of experience. James Porter steps up as Head of Athlete Experience. Retention of this cohort is the highest priority — each EM represents months of ramp-up time and athlete relationship capital. Target: zero involuntary EM departures in H1.

HR Structure: Ellie Dar’s HR coordinator role transitions into a genuine people function — onboarding frameworks, performance review cycles, and culture documentation. The infrastructure built in FY25/26 becomes the foundation for how a 67-person business runs and grows.

Development: Clear progression pathways for EMs from associate through to senior. Becky Price and Ollie Hagar both have the potential to develop into more senior roles with the right structure around them.

Culture: With 43% of the team having joined in FY25/26, codifying what CP+R stands for — how it treats its people, how it makes decisions, what great looks like — becomes essential infrastructure as the business scales toward 80+ people.
