FORWARD LOOK · RECRUITMENT
Recruiting World-Class Employees
Every referral is only as good as the team who services. FY26/27 is about building on the birth of the HR department, PDPs, new job descriptions, expectations, psychological support, management and recruitment. These are the ideas I brought back from the TA Disruptors Summit hosted by Arctic Shores on how we find and select them. A meta-study of TA's at large enterprises revealed the following stats:

What I learned at the summit
Signal quality, not volume
Pattern #1
The biggest destroyer of trust in hiring is candidates who are great interview performaners but not job performers. More applications doesn’t mean more signal — it means more noise to filter. We recruit on demonstrated ability, not polish.
Assess ability to learn
Built for 2030
With 39% of skill sets outdated by 2030, the best predictor of a world-class employee isn’t what they know today — it’s how fast they adapt. We weight learning agility above static experience.
Where this thinking came from
The thinking on this page comes from the TA Disruptors Summit 2026 and Arctic Shores’ State of Volume Hiring 2026 research. The headline lesson for CP+R: as AI floods hiring with noise, the edge goes to teams that select on real, demonstrated ability and the capacity to learn — exactly what separates a good from a world-class one.The human elements that will become the real value: judgement and ethical awareness. Communication and sensemaking. Resilience and emotional regulation. Colloboration and social influence.
World-class coaching is what turns a referral into a retained athlete and the next referral. In FY26/27 we recruit for adaptability over static skill, select on real signal rather than interview polish, and hold every hire to the standard above. The bench we build this year is the income we protect next year.
