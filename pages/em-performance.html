Although we've not yet achieved the performance levels we set out to achieve, I am proud of the togetherness, curiosity and resilience the current EM squad have shown. In any new team, it's about developing training, tactics and repetition before performance starts to show. There have been some fantastic moments to celebrate including the retention of talented coaches Becky Price, Amy Yuill and Hannah Knights. Navigating Megan from an in-person coach, to AX manager to EM. Bringing back a founder member in David Martin and proudly seeing JP step-up and become the EM manager. We built infrastructure, playbooks, a diary structure, an annual leave process, Loomsday, Sharpen the saw meeting, EM controls through Daniella, Emily and Will. All the time reacting to a change in coaching and some tests with availability. I've no doubt that with continuity and retaining this EM, their second season will show an increase in the levels of performance they're all capable of.
