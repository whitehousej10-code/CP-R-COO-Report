The 16.5-day average from referral to BHC booking reflects strong operational throughput in a high-volume year (966 referrals). The 66.9-day or 10 weeks end-to-end journey — referral to payment — is the key commercial metric: every day shortened here accelerates income recognition and improves athlete momentum into the programme. It's currently too slow and has been tested by availbility and athlete behaviour. FY26/27 targets: Ref→BHC 14 days, total journey under 56 days (8-weeks).
