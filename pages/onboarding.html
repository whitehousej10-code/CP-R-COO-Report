966 referrals entered the top of the funnel, up 4.5% on last year. The key friction point is referral-to-ACR at 52.8%. Ellen has been helpful in highlighting the onboarding funnel in her recent Looms. We need better data on why athletes drop off at certain stages of the onboarding funnel, from partnership education, to sales scripts, to EM availability. My sense is that with better data I can work on the education of how the referral sources sell rehab to the patient. In the absence of increased referrals (which we will also pursue), more patients taking their ACRs could have a significant effect.
Of athletes who complete an ACR, 61.5% convert to paid programmes — a strong result for year one of a new EM team, with clear headroom to grow. 99.2% of starters received a programme placement offer.
The action here: categorise all "cold leads" and those that didn't start rehab. Jamie to use the data to feed back to the referral pathway and improve. We've already seen that with less focus on EA responsibilities, Ellen is starting to discover more onboarding trends and find new consultant leads. I have full confidence and optimism in her contribution.
