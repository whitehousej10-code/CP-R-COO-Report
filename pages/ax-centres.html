Strategic priorities · FY26/27

Volume & reliability: increase and stabilise weekly assessment volumes (balancing high/low weeks) and improve cancellation management via pre-assessment screening and stricter policies.

Resilience & experience: invest in equipment resilience and new technologies (e.g. VALD, enhanced CPET capability); enhance patient experience and retention, including upgraded reporting and metrics; and significantly increase Doctify review collection.

People: continue developing staff capability and customer-service excellence.
